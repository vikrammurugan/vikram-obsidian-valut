## LLM
An **LLM (Large Language Model)** is an AI model trained on **massive amounts of text data** so it can **understand, predict, and generate human-like language**. Most modern LLMs are built on the **Transformer architecture**
## Base Model
A **base model** is the **original pre-trained version of an LLM before instruction tuning or chat fine-tuning**.  
It is the **raw foundation** on top of which chatbots, copilots, and domain-specific AI systems are built.
